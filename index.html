About Michelle

I am a graduate from the full-stack web development certification course at UC Davis Extension, and transitioning from my previous career as a biologist. Coming from a science background, I am passionate about the environment and would love to create designs to promote the protection of natural resources.

Creation is a hobby: from art and Photoshop, to furniture restoration, to photography, so building dynamic applications and working with the user experience and user interface is a natural progression!
